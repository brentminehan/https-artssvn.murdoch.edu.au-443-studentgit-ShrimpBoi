This is a folder for your preproduction package
Concept - any concept art to describe your game aesthetic
Design - your quick mechanics/game design doc, your personas, and design pillars
Misc - anything that doesnt fit in another category
Moodboards - moodboards to get the aesthetic feel (based on personas)
Prototype - commit a playable demo (exe + data)
Screenshots - mockup screenshots of what the game could look like
StyleGuide - preliminary style guidelines for art and ui to keep things consistent
Unused - anything that was made but not used.
